This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is processed within the E-Hidromek mobile application (the “App”) provided by HİDROMEK HİDROLİK VE MEKANİK MAKİNA İMALAT SANAYİ VE TİCARET A.Ş. (the “Company”). It is designed to comply with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (“KVKK”) and the privacy disclosure requirements of major app stores.

1) Data Controller

Company: HİDROMEK HİDROLİK VE MEKANİK MAKİNA İMALAT SANAYİ VE TİCARET A.Ş.
Address: Ahi Evran OSB Mahallesi Osmanlı Caddesi No:1, 06935 Sincan, Ankara, Türkiye
Email: info@hidromek.com.tr

2) Personal Data We Process

  • Identity (Name, Surname): For user account and corporate communication.
  • Birthday: With your consent may be shared with other employees to support in‑company celebrations.
  • Location Data: With your consent collected for staff shuttle (transportation) planning and safety.
  • Profile Photo: Displayed in the App to help colleagues recognize each other.
  • Annual Leave Data: Requests, balances and usage to comply with labor law obligations and internal HR processes.

3) Purposes and Legal Bases

  • Performance of a contract / legal obligation (KVKK Art. 5/2‑c, 5/2‑ç): Identity data and annual leave data.
  • Explicit consent (KVKK Art. 5/1): Sharing birthday information with other employees; collecting location data for shuttle services.
  • Legitimate interests (KVKK Art. 5/2‑f): Facilitating internal communication and organization.

4) Processing Requiring Consent

The following processing activities are performed only if you give explicit consent. Refusing consent will not affect your ability to use the App’s core features, and you may withdraw your consent at any time:

  • Sharing your birthday with other employees,
  • Collecting and using your location for shuttle services.

5) Data Sharing and Transfers

  • Internal recipients: HR, managers and units that need access to perform their duties.
  • Other employees: Birthday and profile photo, only if you have consented.
  • Service providers: Identity and/or location data strictly for shuttle organization.
  • Public authorities: Where legally required.
  • International transfers: The Company does not transfer your personal data abroad. If a future transfer becomes necessary, the conditions under KVKK Art. 9 will be fulfilled.

6) Retention

Data is retained for as long as necessary for the purposes described and for the periods required by applicable legislation. Afterwards, it is securely deleted, destroyed or anonymized.

7) Security

We apply administrative and technical measures such as access controls, secure transmission, role‑based authorization and regular audits to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data against unauthorized access or breaches.

8) Your Rights (KVKK Art. 11)

You have the right:

  • To learn whether your data is processed and to request information,
  • To learn the purposes of processing and whether they are used accordingly,
  • To know the third parties to whom data is transferred, domestically or abroad,
  • To request correction if data is incomplete or inaccurate,
  • To request deletion or destruction under KVKK Art. 7,
  • To request notification of such actions to third parties,
  • To object to results arising solely from automated processing,
  • To claim compensation for damages due to unlawful processing.

You can submit your requests by emailing info@hidromek.com.tr or by writing to our registered address. We respond within 10 days in accordance with KVKK Art. 13.

9) Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw your explicit consent at any time via the App settings or by emailing info@hidromek.com.tr. Withdrawal is effective prospectively and does not affect prior processing carried out based on consent.

10) Children’s Privacy

This App is intended only for company employees and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

11) International Users (Optional GDPR Note)

This Policy is primarily prepared in compliance with Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK). If you are located in the European Union, your data will also be processed in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

12) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The latest version is accessible within the App or via the Company’s website.
